Class Structure

  • Frequency: Classes held twice weekly for optimal learning
  • Age Requirement: Minimum 4 years of age
  • Setting: Group classes until Arangetram level
  • Flexibility: Zoom classes available when sick or unavailable

Dress Code

  • Attire: Fitted Churidar, Salwar Kameez, or tights with leggings
  • Dupatta: Long dupatta required for practice
  • Hair: Combed away from face, secured properly
  • Bindi: Traditional bindi on the forehead
  • Footwear: Barefoot for dance practice

School Policies

  • Commitment: We prioritize dedication and open-minded learning
  • Soft Skills: Focus on developing discipline and cultural appreciation
  • Performances: Required participation in annual day/school productions
  • Opportunities: Multiple performance opportunities throughout the year
