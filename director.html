Our Founder and Director

Sharanyaa Ganesan: Educator, Choreographer, and Artist

Director Sharanyaa Ganesan

Sharanyaa Ganesan's Vision

Sharanyaa Ganesan is the **Founder and Artistic Director** of Sruthi School of Arts. Trained in the highly respected **Kalanidhi style** of Bharatanatyam, she has dedicated over two decades to the study and performance of this classical art form.

Her teaching philosophy is centered on the holistic development of the student, combining rigorous technique with the emotional depth of *Abhinaya* (expression). She believes in fostering discipline and respect for the tradition while nurturing the unique artistic voice of each student.

Sharanyaa has choreographed numerous pieces showcased at international dance festivals and has guided over 15 students through their successful Arangetram debuts, cementing her reputation as a dedicated and influential Guru in the community.
