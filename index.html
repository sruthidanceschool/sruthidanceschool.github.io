Our Mission

At Sruthi School of Arts, our mission is to provide an authentic and disciplined education in Bharatanatyam. We focus on cultivating not just technical mastery of the *adavus* and postures, but also the deep artistic and spiritual understanding (*bhava*) essential to the dance form.

Founded by Director [Director's Name], the school is committed to fostering a lifelong appreciation for this classical art among the younger generation, guiding them through every stage, from basic steps to the grand **Arangetram** debut.
