Our Artistic Director

Meet the visionary behind Sruthi School of Arts

Sharanyaa Ganesan

Sharanyaa Ganesan is a distinguished Bharatanatyam artist and the founder of Sruthi School of Arts. With over two decades of dedicated practice and teaching, she has established herself as a respected figure in the classical dance community of the Bay Area.

Quick Facts

  • Founder & Artistic Director
  • 20+ years of teaching experience
  • Trained under renowned gurus
  • Multiple award recipient
  • International performer

Journey & Training

Sharanyaa began her dance journey at the tender age of five, training under the guidance of renowned gurus in the traditional Guru-Shishya parampara. Her rigorous training encompassed not only the technical aspects of Bharatanatyam but also its spiritual and cultural dimensions.

She completed her Arangetram at the age of 16 and continued to deepen her knowledge through advanced training in various aspects of the art form, including choreography, music theory, and dance history.

Expertise & Mastery

  • Classical repertoire and traditional margam
  • Abhinaya (expressive dance) specialization
  • Choreographic innovation while preserving tradition
  • Music theory and rhythm (Tala) understanding
  • Cultural and historical context of dance pieces

Teaching Philosophy

As an educator, Sharanyaa believes in nurturing each student's individual potential while maintaining the authentic essence of Bharatanatyam. Her teaching methodology combines traditional techniques with modern pedagogical approaches, ensuring students develop both technical proficiency and artistic expression.

She emphasizes the importance of discipline, cultural respect, and personal growth through dance, preparing students not just to perform, but to become ambassadors of this beautiful art form.

Notable Achievements

  • Founded Sruthi School of Arts in 2010
  • Trained over 200 students to date
  • Conducted 15+ successful Arangetrams
  • Regular performer at cultural festivals
  • Featured artist at major Bay Area events
  • Guest lecturer at universities
